🌱 How to Play Climate Heroes

🎯 Mission

Be the first Climate Hero to reach Net Zero by implementing sustainable solutions and overcoming environmental challenges. Start with a carbon footprint of 100 and reduce it through strategic actions!

🎲 Gameplay

  • Players take turns rolling the dice (1-6) to move across the board
  • Each space you land on has different effects on your carbon footprint
  • Track your progress: position, carbon level, and climate actions taken
  • First player to reach the finish line (space 25) becomes the Climate Hero!

Learn about actual solutions like solar energy, wind farms, electric vehicles, reforestation, sustainable agriculture, and more. Each action you take in the game represents real-world climate initiatives that help reduce carbon emissions!
